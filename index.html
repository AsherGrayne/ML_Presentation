Training Pipeline & Methodology

Pipeline Flow

Raw Data ‚Üí Cleaning ‚Üí Feature Extraction ‚Üí Normalization ‚Üí Personalization ‚Üí Training

Personalization Strategies

  • User-Specific Baselines: Personal normal ranges
  • Z-Score Normalization: Relative to personal baseline
  • Transfer Learning: Population model fine-tuned per user
  • Online Learning: Continuous model updates

Validation Approaches

  • Cross-Validation: User-independent splits
  • Temporal Validation: Train on past, test on future
  • Clinical Validation: Compare with clinical assessments
